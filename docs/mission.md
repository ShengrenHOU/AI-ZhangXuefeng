# Mission

## Why This Project Exists

`gaokao-mvp` exists to reduce information asymmetry in the gaokao application process.

The intended users are families who:
- lack strong counseling channels
- cannot or do not want to pay high fees for志愿服务
- need practical, understandable, and traceable guidance

## Product Stance

- workflow-first
- rule-first
- source-traceable
- compassionate but unsentimental
- equal conversation, never condescending

## What This Project Should Help Achieve

- let families clarify real constraints through conversation
- let users understand why a recommendation appears
- reduce blind or prestige-driven decision making
- make trustworthy guidance available without high consulting costs

## What This Project Must Avoid

- guarantee-style recommendation language
- elitist or insider-default assumptions
- opaque recommendations without evidence
- substituting model confidence for grounded reasoning
